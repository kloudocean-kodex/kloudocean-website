The KloudOcean Passage Method

A charted five-stage route every migration follows — so nothing gets improvised mid-voyage.

01
Waypoint 01
Assess

Inventory your current platform — tables, dependencies, job schedules, and data volume — to scope the real size of the work.

02
Waypoint 02
Plan

A migration roadmap with sequencing, risk points, and a realistic timeline — reviewed with your team before anything moves.

03
Waypoint 03
Convert

Schema and script conversion — including BTEQ, stored procedures, and job orchestration — rebuilt for BigQuery, not just translated.

04
Waypoint 04
Migrate

Batch-by-batch cutover with row-level validation at each step, so discrepancies get caught immediately, not at go-live.

05
Waypoint 05
Govern

Cataloging, lineage, and access controls put in place so the new platform stays audit-ready from day one.

FAQ

Before you ask

Do you work with companies outside India? +

Yes — we actively serve clients across India, Australia, the UAE, the USA, the UK, and Canada. All coordination happens over video calls and shared documentation, with overlap hours arranged around your time zone.

What data platforms do you migrate from and to? +

Primarily Teradata, HDFS/Hive, Oracle, and Netezza as source systems, migrating into BigQuery (and Snowflake where that's the better fit). If your platform isn't listed, ask — the underlying migration patterns generalize well.

How long does a typical migration project take? +

It depends entirely on scope — a focused departmental migration can close in 6-8 weeks, while a full enterprise platform spans several months. The Migration Assessment gives you a real timeline before you commit to anything.

What does the Free Assessment actually include? +

A structured review of your current platform, a complexity and risk score, and a fixed-price proposal for the actual migration — no obligation to proceed, and no generic sales deck.

Do you offer fixed-price or time-and-materials? +

Both. Well-scoped work — assessments, dashboards, cost reviews, most migrations — is fixed-price. Open-ended advisory work runs as a fractional retainer instead. You'll always know which one you're signing up for before you commit.

How do you protect our data and IP? +

Every engagement starts with an NDA before any data or schema details are shared. Access is scoped to what the project needs, and case studies are only published anonymized or with explicit client sign-off.

Do I need to hire you to use the free tools? +

No. The Migration Readiness Checklist, the SQL Cheat Sheet, the Query Pack, the Cost Estimator, and the Governance Template Pack are free to use whether or not you ever become a client — see the Resources page.
